    <div class="arxiv-section-title">Abstract</div>
    <div class="arxiv-abstract">
      We propose CipherTune, a training-free calibration method for large language models that corrects prediction errors by identifying and adjusting low-dimensional error subspaces in the model's representation space. Without any parameter updates, CipherTune detects systematic error patterns in the latent representations and applies targeted corrections through subspace projection, achieving effective calibration with minimal computational overhead.
