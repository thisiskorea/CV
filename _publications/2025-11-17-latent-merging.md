    <div class="arxiv-section-title">Abstract</div>
    <div class="arxiv-abstract">
      We propose Latent Merging, a dynamic and reversible method for composing multiple large language models in hidden state space. Unlike conventional model merging approaches that permanently alter model weights, Latent Merging operates at the representation level, enabling flexible composition and decomposition of model capabilities at inference time. This approach allows for task-specific combination of specialized models while preserving the original models intact, offering a practical and efficient alternative to traditional merging and ensemble methods.
