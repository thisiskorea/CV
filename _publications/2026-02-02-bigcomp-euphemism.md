    <div class="arxiv-section-title">Abstract</div>
    <div class="arxiv-abstract">
      This paper analyzes how multilingual euphemistic coating techniques can be used to circumvent the safety mechanisms of large language models. We design euphemistic coating methods that exploit linguistic characteristics across multiple languages to bypass LLM safety filters, and experimentally evaluate the vulnerability of various LLMs in multilingual contexts. Our findings highlight critical gaps in current multilingual safety alignment and suggest directions for more robust cross-lingual safety mechanisms.
