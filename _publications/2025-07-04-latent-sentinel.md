    <div class="arxiv-section-title">Abstract</div>
    <div class="arxiv-abstract">
      We propose Latent Sentinel, a real-time jailbreak detection framework that leverages layer-wise probing classifiers to identify adversarial prompts in large language models. By monitoring the internal representations at each transformer layer, Latent Sentinel detects jailbreak attempts before the model generates harmful outputs. Our approach achieves high detection accuracy with minimal latency overhead, enabling practical deployment for LLM safety without modifying the underlying model architecture.
