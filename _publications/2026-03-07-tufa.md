    <div class="arxiv-section-title">Abstract</div>
    <div class="arxiv-abstract">
      We propose TuFA (Tuning-Free Adaptation), a method that adapts large language models to downstream tasks without any parameter updates. TuFA projects LLM hidden representations into task-relevant latent subspaces and applies lightweight classification heads, enabling efficient task adaptation purely through the model's existing representation space. This approach eliminates the need for fine-tuning while maintaining competitive performance across diverse NLP tasks.
