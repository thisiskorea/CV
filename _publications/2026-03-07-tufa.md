    <div class="arxiv-section-title">Abstract</div>
    <div class="arxiv-abstract">This paper proposes TuFA (Tuning-Free Adaptation), a method that adapts large language models to downstream tasks without any parameter tuning. TuFA projects LLM hidden representations into task-specific latent spaces and applies lightweight classification heads, enabling zero-shot task adaptation with minimal computational overhead. The method leverages the rich representational capacity of pretrained LLMs while avoiding the cost of fine-tuning, providing a practical solution for resource-constrained deployment scenarios.</div>
