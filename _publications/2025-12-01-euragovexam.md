    <div class="arxiv-section-title">Abstract</div>
    <div class="arxiv-abstract">
      EuraGovExam is a multilingual multimodal benchmark constructed from real-world civil service examinations across multiple countries. The benchmark evaluates large language models on diverse reasoning tasks including textual comprehension, visual understanding, and domain-specific knowledge across languages and modalities. By leveraging authentic government examination questions, EuraGovExam provides a rigorous and practical evaluation framework that reflects real-world complexity and cultural diversity in LLM assessment.
