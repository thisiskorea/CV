    <div class="arxiv-section-title">Abstract</div>
    <div class="arxiv-abstract">
      This paper proposes a hybrid approach that combines recurrent neural network structures with attention mechanisms for early prediction of real-time streaming text. By leveraging the sequential processing capability of recurrent architectures and the contextual focus of attention mechanisms, the proposed method achieves accurate predictions with minimal input tokens from streaming text data. Experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the hybrid approach in various real-time text prediction scenarios.
