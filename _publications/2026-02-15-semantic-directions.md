    <div class="arxiv-section-title">Abstract</div>
    <div class="arxiv-abstract">
      This paper investigates how semantic directions emerge within the representation spaces of large language models through the lens of sparse token mixtures. We show that meaningful semantic directions arise naturally from the sparse combination of token representations, revealing a structured geometric organization in LLM hidden states. Our analysis demonstrates that these emergent directions encode interpretable linguistic and conceptual information, providing new insights into how transformers organize knowledge in their internal representations.
